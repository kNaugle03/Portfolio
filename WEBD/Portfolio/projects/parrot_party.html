Overview

This is one of my favorite assignments we got this year, even though it was one of the first. This assignment gave me the opportunity to do what I love most, coding a cool page from a mockup, and watching that design come to life in my browser! This is really when I realized that I enjoy the design aspect of web development as much as I enjoy the architectural aspect of it. I've included this piece because I'm very proud of the way it turned out, it's really almost a 1-to-1.

Challenges I Faced

Even though I did have some prior experience with coding from a mock, and with some slightly more advanced CSS tricks, some of the aspects of this mock did give me some trouble. Recreating the 3 Card grid at the end of the page was very challenging, and getting every piece of content to line up perfectly inside it's card was definitely a trying task. But I found that overcoming those challenges really gave me more confidence in both my code, and my design sense.

What I Learned

I gathered some really cool CSS tips from this assignment, and I also got some really great resources for any further learning in the CSS/design world! I also learned that I could sit down and play with CSS for hours on end. This assignment taught me the value of having a strong idea for design before even beginning to think about code, and that just designing a simple mockup can make your life SO much easier!
